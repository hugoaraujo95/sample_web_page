Giles Corey

Giles Corey is acoustic music about history, suicide, and ghosts. Giles Corey began as a collection of acoustic songs written by Dan Barrett (of Have A Nice Life and Nahvalr). “Trust me, after you nightmares, dried eyes and eased stomach you will thank me, and most importantly you will thank Dan Barrett.” – Heavy Boots Music
